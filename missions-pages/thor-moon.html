Thor

Deep Space Lunar Descent Mission

Mission Overview

Thor is StarReach Aerospace’s flagship lunar descent mission, designed to establish a permanent human presence on the Moon. This mission will deliver modular habitat modules capable of supporting extended lunar operations and laying the groundwork for future exploration.

Thor is StarReach Aerospace’s revolutionary lunar descent and habitation platform. Unlike the Apollo landers, Thor delivers modular landers shaped like standardized shipping containers capable of carrying both personnel and heavy payloads to the Moon's surface. These modules allow extended-duration work missions, habitat construction, and resource return missions. They also serve as the foundational units for a potential permanent lunar base.

The Thor mission will be carried to the Moon aboard the Liliana-Class Luna Transport , a next-generation StarReach heavy lifter named in honor of quiet strength and enduring spirit. Vess was engineered for deep-space cargo operations, including delivery of lunar habitat modules and heavy infrastructure containers. As the sister ship to Thor, the Liliana-class symbolizes the foundation, care, and vision needed to build a permanent human presence on the Moon. The Luna Transport always shows the strength needed even after the Thor mission is complete.

Technical Specifications

Thor modules feature advanced systems designed for lunar operations, logistics support, and human habitation in harsh environments.

Structure

Pressurized aluminum-lithium monocoque container

Propulsion

Throttleable methane/LOX descent engine

Power System

Deployable solar arrays with 120-day battery backup

Life Support

Closed-loop system with 3 redundancy tiers

Comm System

High-gain array with Earth sync and relay to Odin’s Eye

Thermal Regulation

Phase-change and radiative panels

Meet Autonomous Mission Yield

Each Thor module is equipped with Autonomous Mission Yield (AMY), an advanced autonomous system that watches over every heartbeat, every CO₂ level, every breach warning. If the hull is compromised, if power drops below survivable thresholds, or if radiation spikes—AMY doesn’t hesitate. She yields control, seals off danger, routes emergency power, and buys astronauts minutes that make the difference between headlines and homecoming.

Thor is the body. AMY is the nervous system. Without Thor’s modular design, AMY has nowhere to live. She relies on Thor and she protects Thor.

Protect Thor to protect Amy — and everything she protects in turn.
