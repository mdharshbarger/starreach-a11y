Mission Architecture

Tailored planning for orbital, lunar, and deep space objectives.

Mission Design Map

Orbital Earth Imaging: High-resolution multispectral systems designed for climate monitoring, urban analysis, and disaster detection. Optimized for sun-synchronous orbit.
Lunar Supply Transport: Modular payload delivery system for precision landings. Features thermal shielding, descent thrusters, and payload bay optimized for lunar gravity and terrain.
Asteroid Survey Probe: Deep-space radiation-hardened platform with low-thrust ion propulsion and robotic sampling arm. Used for mapping near-Earth objects.
Interplanetary Crew Habitat: Closed-loop life support with modular shielding and spin-gravity docking systems. Designed for 24+ month missions to Mars and beyond.
