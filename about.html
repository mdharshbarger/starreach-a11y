About StarReach Aerospace

From Imagination to Ignition

Our Founder: RH Locke

StarReach Aerospace was born from a single question: What if the final frontier wasn’t final at all?

RH Locke didn’t come from legacy aerospace... He was a dreamer with a pen, a vision, and a firm belief that space exploration belongs to all of us.

His early designs became the backbone of Hermes Network, Odin’s Eye, and Project Thor.

More than a founder, RH Locke is a storyteller... Something shared. Something human.

StarReach exists because one person looked up—and decided not to wait for permission.
