About StarReach Aerospace

From Imagination to Ignition

Our Founder: RH Locke

StarReach Aerospace was born from a single question: What if the final frontier wasn’t final at all?

RH Locke didn’t come from legacy aerospace. He wasn’t backed by billionaires or national agencies. He didn't start with selling cars or books. He was a dreamer with a pen, a vision, and a firm belief that space exploration belongs to all of us.

In a converted workshop just outside Austin, Locke began sketching out ideas not just for ships, but for systems—reusable, modular, human-centric platforms for space logistics, science, and long-term habitation. His early designs became the backbone of what would one day become the Hermes Network, Odin’s Eye, and the crowning jewel of our program, Project Thor.

More than a founder, RH Locke is a storyteller. He reminds us that every mission we launch is a chapter in something larger. Something shared. Something human.

StarReach exists because one person looked up—and decided not to wait for permission.
