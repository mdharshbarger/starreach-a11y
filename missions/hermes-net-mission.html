Hermes Net

LEO Communications Array

Mission Overview

Hermes Net is StarReach Aerospace’s low Earth orbit (LEO) satellite array dedicated to revolutionizing global communications. By creating a mesh of interconnected satellites, Hermes Net delivers ultra-fast, low-latency connectivity even in the most remote regions of Earth. This constellation is the foundation of tomorrow’s interplanetary data relay. Hermes Net launches will utilize our next-generation reusable launch vehicle—designed for rapid turnaround and frequent deployment. This enables accelerated deployment of the full array following the success of the initial launch.
