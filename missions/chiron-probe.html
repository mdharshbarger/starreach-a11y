Chiron Probe

StarReach’s first step toward interstellar exploration.

Mission Overview

The Chiron Probe is a bold vision—StarReach Aerospace’s commitment to extending humanity’s reach beyond the inner solar system. Though still in the early concept phase, Chiron represents the long-term ambition to send scientific payloads to the Kuiper Belt and beyond. This mission stands not as a present initiative, but as a promise: StarReach will not stop at orbit, Moon, or Mars. We aim to explore the edge of our solar system—and someday, what lies beyond it.

While current resources are focused on critical near-term goals like lunar logistics, orbital platforms, and Martian cargo architecture, Chiron reminds us of the destination ahead. It’s a placeholder for destiny.
