FAQ

Are StarReach Aeronautics launches open to the public?
Yes! You can request a launch pass by visiting the individual mission page for the launch you're interested in.
Where are your launch sites?
Our primary launch site is Cape Orion, FL. We also have facilities in the Arctic and at sea for specialized missions.
How can I watch a launch if I can’t attend in person?
All major StarReach Aeronautics launches are livestreamed with commentary, mission updates, and multi-angle views. Visit the mission page or subscribe to our YouTube and social media channels for access.
Can I tour your facilities?
Public tours of Cape Orion are offered quarterly and must be scheduled in advance. Arctic and sea-based launch platforms are restricted for safety, security, and environmental reasons.
Do you offer internships or educational programs?
Yes! We have internship opportunities for college students and host educational outreach events throughout the year. Visit our Careers or Outreach page for current openings and program information.
What safety precautions are in place for launch viewing?
Launch passes include safety instructions and designated viewing areas. We work closely with local authorities to ensure a safe and unforgettable experience for all attendees.
